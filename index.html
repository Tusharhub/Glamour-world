Frequently Asked Questions

The recovery time for acne scar treatment varies depending on the specific treatment used. In general, patients can expect some redness and swelling immediately after treatment, which typically subsides within a few days to a week. Complete healing and optimal results may take several weeks to months.

A chemical peel is a skin-resurfacing procedure in which a chemical solution is applied to the skin to remove the top layers. This promotes the growth of new, healthier skin cells and improves skin texture and appearance.

The results from Botox and filler treatments vary depending on the individual, the treatment area, and the specific products used. In general, patients may start to see initial results within a few days to a week after treatment, with full results typically appearing within two weeks to a month.

The number of sessions needed for laser hair removal varies depending on factors such as hair type, skin type, and the area being treated. In general, most patients require multiple sessions spaced several weeks apart to achieve optimal results. Typically, 6-8 sessions are recommended for best outcomes.

Mole removal is typically done through surgical excision, where the mole is cut out using a scalpel or other surgical instruments. In some cases, laser therapy or other techniques may also be used to remove moles.
