Chemical Peels in Rajendra Place: Benefits, Cost & Recovery

Chemical peels have become one of the most effective skin treatments to address pigmentation, acne, dullness, and early signs of aging. If you're considering chemical peels in Rajendra Place, this guide breaks down the benefits, cost, and recovery process.

What are Chemical Peels?

A chemical peel is a technique used to improve skin texture by applying a chemical solution that exfoliates and eventually peels off the top layers of the skin. The new skin that forms is smoother and brighter.

Benefits of Chemical Peels

  • Brightens Dull Skin: A great solution for tired and uneven skin tone.
  • Reduces Acne and Scars: Salicylic acid peels help reduce breakouts and fade old acne scars.
  • Anti-Aging: Reduces the appearance of fine lines and age spots.
  • Minimizes Pores: Leaves your skin looking smoother and clearer.
  • Boosts Collagen: Stimulates skin cell turnover and regeneration.

Cost of Chemical Peels in Rajendra Place

At Glamour World Laser Hair and Skin Clinic, chemical peels start at just ₹1,500 and can go up to ₹6,000 depending on the type of peel and the skin condition being treated.

Recovery and Aftercare

  • Mild Peels: Minimal peeling, light redness, recovery in 1-2 days.
  • Medium Peels: Peeling lasts 3-5 days, skin may be red for a week.
  • Deep Peels: Require longer downtime and should be done under expert supervision.

Avoid sun exposure, use sunscreen, and follow the post-treatment routine recommended by your dermatologist.
