Experience the Hair PRP Treatment Process

Embark on a journey to restored hair with our specialized Hair PRP treatment procedure, meticulously designed to target hair loss concerns with precision.

Hair PRP treatment involves injecting platelet-rich plasma into the scalp, where it stimulates hair follicles, promotes hair growth, and improves hair density.

The number of sessions needed varies depending on individual factors such as the extent of hair loss and desired outcomes. Typically, a series of 3-6 sessions spaced several weeks apart is recommended for optimal results.

Yes, Hair PRP treatment is generally safe as it utilizes your body's own platelets. However, mild discomfort or temporary side effects such as minor swelling or redness at the injection sites may occur, but they typically resolve within a few days.

Hair PRP treatment offers numerous benefits, including increased hair thickness, improved hair density, enhanced hair quality, and stimulation of dormant hair follicles. It also aids in halting hair loss and promoting natural hair growth.

Following Hair PRP treatment, you may experience minor discomfort or redness at the injection sites, but this typically resolves within a few hours to days. There is minimal downtime, and you can resume your regular activities immediately after the procedure.
