Experience the Chemical Peel Process

Embark on a journey to revitalized skin with our multi-step chemical peel procedure, meticulously designed to exfoliate, stimulate cellular renewal, and unveil a more youthful appearance.

Chemical peel treatments offer a myriad of benefits, including deep exfoliation, improved skin texture, and reduced signs of aging. They can also help diminish the appearance of acne scars and promote collagen production for firmer, more youthful-looking skin.

The duration of a chemical peel treatment typically ranges from 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the type and depth of the peel administered by our skilled professionals.

Yes, chemical peels can be tailored to suit various skin types and concerns, including sensitive skin. Our experienced clinicians will assess your skin's condition and recommend the most suitable peel for optimal results.

The frequency of chemical peel treatments depends on your skin's condition and desired outcome. Generally, a series of peels spaced 2 to 4 weeks apart is recommended for optimal results, followed by maintenance treatments as needed.

Following a chemical peel, you may experience mild redness, peeling, or sensitivity, which typically subsides within a few days to a week, depending on the depth of the peel. Our skincare experts will provide post-treatment care instructions to ensure your comfort and optimal results.
