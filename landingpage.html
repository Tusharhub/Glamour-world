Hair patches, also known as hair systems or toupees, are custom-made hairpieces designed to cover balding or thinning areas. They are attached to the scalp using various methods, such as adhesive tapes or clips, and blend seamlessly with natural hair for a realistic appearance.

The longevity of hair patches depends on factors such as the quality of the material used, maintenance routine, and frequency of wear. On average, well-made hair patches can last anywhere from several months to a few years with proper care.
